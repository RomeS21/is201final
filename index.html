About

From my story to yours...

  • 2001-2021

    My Humble Beginnings

    All growing up I loved expressing my creativity through art and the camera. I once cried in the car when I was 7 years old because my sister wouldn't let me have the camera to take pictures on our drive through Florida.

  • 2021

    Discovering my Passion

    I didn't begin taking my love for photography and videography seriously until 2021 when I began teaching myself the art of being behind the camera through online courses and Youtube.

  • 2023

    Transition to Business

    In June 2023 I took on a few small business clients, managing their social media pages and creating marketing content. Here is where I began learning how to create shortform videos that capture attention as well as how to take product photos for promotions.

  • 2024

    Always Learning

    I continue to learn valuable skills in photo and video editing as well as how to seize the moment in the very best ways. I have filmed business videos, wedding events and travel videos, as well as taken many couples photos. I look forward to bringing your vision to life!
